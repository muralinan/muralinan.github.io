Brand Valuation

We all know that 'Brand' is an in-tangible asset and is hard to assign a dollar amount to the brand.  There is a London-based consulting company, Brand Finance that specializes in putting a dollar value to the brand. There is an ISO standard 10668:2010 specifies requirements for procedures and methods of monetary brand value measurement. http://www.brandfinance.com/images/upload/global_500_iso10668_310310.pdf"
